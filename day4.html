VIRAT KOHLI

Virat Kohli is an Indian international cricketer and the former captain of the Indian national cricket team. He currently represents Royal Challengers Bangalore in the IPL and Delhi in domestic cricket. Kohli is widely regarded e of the greatest batsmen in the history of cricket, and the best of 21st century.
